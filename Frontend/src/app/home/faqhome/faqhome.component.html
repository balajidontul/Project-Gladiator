FAQs

What is the Objective of this scheme?
The objective of the scheme is to promote availability of safe and conveniently located accommodation for working women, with day care facility for their children,wherever possible, in urban, semi urban, or even rural areas where employment opportunity for women exist.
What categories of working women and their children are being covered under this Scheme?
Working women, who may be single, widowed, divorced, separated, married but whose husband or immediate family does not reside in the same city/area. preference may be given to women from disadvantaged sections of the society.
Is there any provision for reservation of seats for physically challenged people?
Yes there is provision for reservation of seats for physically challenged beneficiaries.
Does women under training eligible for this scheme?
Yes, Women who are under training for job are eligible but provided that the total training period does not exceed one year.
What is the total capacity if women under training under this scheme?
The number of women under training for job should not exceed 30% of the total capacity.
What is the maximum age for Childers for accommodation under this scheme?
Girls up to the age of 18 years and boys up to the age of 5 years, accompanying working mothers will be provided accommodation, with their mothers.
Does working mothers are eligible for this scheme?
Yes, Working mothers may also avail of the services of the Day Care Centre, as provided under the scheme.
What is the gross income limit for entitlement of hostel under this scheme?
Working Women are entitled to hostel facilities provided their gross income does not exceed Rs. 50,000/- consolidated (gross) per month in metropolitan cities, or Rs 35,000/- consolidated (gross) per month, in any other place.
